Fun facts about Okapis according to nationalgeographic.com


  1. SIZE: Length, eight feet; height, five feet

  2. The okapi looks more like a cross between a deer and a zebra

  3. The okapi lives among dense flora in the rainforest.

  4. Active during the day, the elusive okapi prefers to be alone.

  5. WEIGHT: 440 to 660 pounds
