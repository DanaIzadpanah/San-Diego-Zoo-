Fun facts about Koalas according to wwf.org.au


  • koalas are a national symbol of Australia’s unique wildlife

  • Koalas survive on a diet of eucalyptus leaves and can eat up to a kilogram a day!

  • Koalas can sleep up to 18 hours a day

  • It was thought that koalas didn’t need to drink because of the moisture they get from munching on juicy eucalyptus leaves all day.
